About Us

Pet4Sitter is a platform created by students of Florida Universitaria as their final project, dedicated to connecting pet owners with reliable caretakers.
They offer high-quality services for dog walking, cat sitting, and fish feeding, among other needs. We have designed a secure and user-friendly platform to ensure a worry-free experience when finding passionate and trustworthy caretakers.
Pet4Sitter focuses on providing personalized service for each pet and owner, ensuring peace of mind knowing that pets are well cared for. Join Pet4Sitter to ensure the best care for your beloved furry or scaly friends.
